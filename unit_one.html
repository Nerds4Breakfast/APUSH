Unit 1: 1491-1607

Native American Societies

Here are the main goals for studying Unit 1:

  • Explain context for European encounters in Americas from 1491-1607
  • Explain how and why various populations before Europeans interacted with environment
  • Explain cause/effect of exporation and conquest
  • Explain cause/effect of Columbian Exchange and its effects on Europe and America after 1492
  • Explain how growth of Spanish Empire in North America shaped development of social and economic structures over time
  • Explain how European and Native American perspectives changed
  • Explain development of Transatlantic voyage and its effects
